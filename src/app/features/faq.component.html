Frequently Asked Questions

Please search for your Question

01. Why we need to choose this program for our students?
  • To introduce a technological world to your students
  • To develop their interpersonal & communication skills.
  • To give them a national platform for competition.
  • To provide them a carrier guidance.
  • Your institute will be recognized as the “Zonal Centre” at pan India level.
02. How our school or college be Zonal centre for Techradiance’19?

School or college need to send us the consent letter through mail with the institute letter head.

03. What is the eligibility criteria for students?

School: Students from class 6 th to 12 th can participate in the event depending upon topic. College: Any student from 1 st year to final year can be a part of the event. Outside: Any enthusiastic person or teacher can be a part of the Event.

04. What would be the probable dates or month for the Zonal Round?

School or college need to decide the dates according to their academic calendar.

05. How many topics we’ll be able to select?

Your college or school will be the Zonal centre. So as a Zonal centre you can select any topic. Every topic should have minimum count strength.

06. What do you mean by Zonal Centre?

Zonal Centre is a place where students will attend the Techradiance event at the Zonal level.

07. What are the roles & responsibility as a Zonal Centre?
  • Seminar hall/classroom having enough capacity to conduct hands-on-session for all participants.
  • A Good quality public addressing system ideally two cordless Mic. will be required.
  • Projector/ Screen along with black/white board for teaching and presentation purpose.
  • Hospitality of our Technical Trainer for respective duration.
  • Pick & drop facility from the nearest bus stop / Railway station / Airport.
08. What about the maximum count for one workshop?

There is no limit about the maximum count. It will be a great event at your centre if participant will be more. Your start rating will increase towards Techradiance event.

09. How you are going to select the students for finale?

Resource person will select the students on the behalf of the competition that will held after workshop. Competition will be related to the workshop content only.

10. How many students you are going to select for finale?

Techradiances’ team will select 30 % of the total participant.

11. Is there any possibilities to have a special topic in our college/school?

Yes, you can conduct the topic apart from the list. School or college need to provide us the topic name or idea of the workshop.
