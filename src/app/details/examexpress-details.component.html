Scope of online exam

Choosing a course is very simple but getting into it one should pass entrance exam. On the basis of entrance examination suitable candidates are selected and on the basis of score and ranking obtained by the candidate in entrance examination, Colleges and institutes provide admission into courses. The entrance exam has various scopes since it provide chance of forming well-built educational foundation in present competitive market. The main purpose of conducting entrance exam is to judge the student ability, sharpness, knowledge etc. The aptitude of the student is tested in entrance exam.
